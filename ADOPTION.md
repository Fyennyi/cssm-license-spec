# Adopting CSSM Unlimited License

1. Copy the relevant license file (see `LICENSE-2.0.txt` or `LICENSE-1.0.txt`) to your project as `LICENSE`.
2. Ensure you replace `[YEAR]` and `[AUTHOR NAME]`.
3. Use the [header template](../examples/header-template.txt) in your code.
4. Mention CSSM Unlimited License in your `package.json` or `README`.
5. For modifications, always distribute under CSSM Unlimited License v2.0.

**Questions?** Open an [Issue](https://github.com/Fyennyi/cssm-unlimited-license/issues) or visit [cssmp.pp.ua](https://cssmp.pp.ua).
