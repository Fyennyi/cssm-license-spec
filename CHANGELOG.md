# CSSM Unlimited License — Changelog

## [2.0.0] – 2024-11-09

### Added
- Explicit **copyleft requirement**: All modifications and derivative works must be licensed under CSSM Unlimited License v2.0.
- Detailed section on license termination and restoration of rights after violation correction.
- The right for the Author to verify license compliance and require compensation for damages.
- Clauses requiring users to report discovered errors and unauthorized access.
- Obligation for users to maintain authorship and copyright attribution in all copies and derivative works.
- Clear distribution rules: modified versions must indicate changes and include contact information for modifier.
- Regular notification of users about license changes, including sufficient review time.
- Users' obligation to indemnify the Author against claims related to software use.
- Obligatory notification about license changes on the Author's official site or documentation.
- Clarified the process and rules for restoring rights after a violation, before it is discovered by the Author.

### Changed
- All references to "Licensor" now use "Author" for consistency.
- License text edited for clarity and legal precision, including definitions.
- Reworded disclaimer section to clarify "as is" provision and risks.
- Improved language regarding commercial use and compensation.
- Termination conditions improved: license is automatically revoked if violation occurs, process for restoring rights described.

### Removed
- Informal, non-binding recommendations and good wishes from the v1.0 license (such as "wish you success", "bring popularity").
- Beta testing provisions: now out of scope of core license text.
- Game-specific paragraphs (e.g., loss of worlds, creating backups for games).

### Deprecated
- v1.0's approach to user feedback and ratings (*now replaced by mandatory feedback in v2.0*).
- Data protection, employee obligations, and third-party document technologies — now out of scope.

### Compatibility
- v2.0 is **not backward compatible**: all new modifications must use v2.0 terms (not v1.0).

## [1.0.0] – 2019-12-15

### Initial Release
- Original CSSM Unlimited License introducing free use for any purpose, basic attribution requirement, and good-will recommendations.
- No explicit copyleft or mandatory feedback clauses.
- Simple modification and distribution terms.
- Included informal, game-specific provisions and recommendations for data backup.
- Provided for employee beta testing, protection of user data, and partner document technologies.
